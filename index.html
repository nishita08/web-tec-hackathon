The Bangalore Eatery. A multicuisine family restaurant offering a wide variety of food where every flavor tells a story. A taste that you’ll remember.

  • Where the Southern charm meets the California vibe.
  • A time-honored experience with a modern twist.
  • A place where food, design, and ambiance come together to create a memorable experience. The first bite will blow your mind. The second will transport you to another dimension.

    • The Bangalore Eatery a farm-to-table restaurant that uses all-natural ingredients. An experience you won’t forget. A small, intimate, and inviting space for an unforgettable meal.

Why The Bangalore Eatery?

The Bangalore Eatery strives to source local, sustainable and organic when possible. We work hard to source premium ingredients and we cook everything from scratch with love. We hope that you find value and feel a sense of comfort in knowing that we aim to get better everyday at doing what is important to us.
